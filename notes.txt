1.1 Update!
- Changed and improved application component styles.
- Removed the ability to change the application theme style, now the Fusion style will always be used by default.
- Fixed a bug where switching to "Top of all windows" mode hid the window from the user's view.
- Added the ability to enable the tray when closing the main window.
- Fixed a bug that when opening a folder, other multimedia from other folders in the open folder were added to the playlist
- Added the ability to drag and drop your media files and folders on your pc into the app window.
- Other changes and improvements.
